# Case study template

## Topic

State the topic in one line.

---

## Why this matters

Explain why this topic matters for one or more of:
- implementation
- product behavior
- compatibility
- testing
- innovation

---

## Question

What exact question is this case study trying to answer?

---

## Evidence sources

Classify the evidence used.

- [ ] Official public source
- [ ] Repository experiment
- [ ] Strong inference
- [ ] Open question

List the concrete sources or experiments used.

---

## Verified

List only the points strongly supported by the evidence.

---

## Observed behavior

If experiments were run, describe the observed behavior here.
Include enough context to understand what was tested.

---

## What remains uncertain

Be explicit.
This is often the most valuable section for future work.

---

## Likely implications

What probably follows for design or implementation?
Keep these separate from the verified section.

---

## Fringe cases / failure modes

Ask at least some of these where relevant:
- what happens with zero targets?
- what happens with multiple instances?
- what happens when selection changes?
- what happens on save/reload?
- what happens under automation?
- what happens across versions or platforms?

---

## Smallest useful experiment

What is the smallest additional experiment that would most reduce uncertainty?

---

## Recommended practice

What should the repo, code, or product approach do differently because of this case study?

---

## Confidence

Choose one and justify briefly:
- High
- Medium
- Low

---

## Follow-up tasks

List any concrete next steps.
